Poland Student Visa Requirements

1. Visa Application Form: Completed and signed visa application form. If you are a minor, it must be signed by both parents.

2. Passport: A valid travel document (passport) which is valid for at least three months after your planned departure from Poland, has at least two blank pages, and was issued within the last ten years.

3. Photographs: Two recent passport-sized biometric photographs.

4. Proof of Visa Payment: Evidence of the visa application fee payment.

5. Medical Insurance: Travel medical insurance with a coverage of at least EUR 30,000. It should cover medical emergencies, urgent hospital treatment, or death.

6. Proof of Financial Means: Evidence that you have sufficient funds to cover your living expenses while in Poland. This can include:

  • Bank account statements from the last three months.
  • Salary slips or proof of income.
  • Documents confirming a scholarship or student loan.
  • Notarized letter of sponsorship if someone else is funding your stay, along with their proof of income.

7. Proof of Accommodation: Documents showing where you will stay in Poland, such as a dormitory agreement, lease, or a hotel reservation.

8. Letter of Acceptance: An official acceptance letter from a Polish educational institution.

9. Flight Reservation: Proof of your intended travel dates, including a return ticket.

10. Proof of Residency: For third-country nationals, proof of legal residence in the country from which you are applying.

11. Additional Documents: Any other documents that may be required by the specific consulate, such as previous visas, family registration certificates, or proof of enrollment in specific programs.
